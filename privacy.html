Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Welcome to CogAT Learning Adventure. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe learning environment for children. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information through cookies and similar technologies:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, clicks, and interactions
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device type
  • Location Data: General geographic location (city/country level)
  • Learning Progress: Module completion, scores, and lesson progress (stored locally on your device)

1.2 Information You Provide

Our application stores learning progress data locally in your browser. This includes:

  • Completed modules and lessons
  • Quiz scores and performance metrics
  • Personal preferences and settings
Note: This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings.

2. How We Use Information

2.1 Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This helps us:

  • Improve the educational content and user experience
  • Understand which modules are most popular
  • Identify technical issues and improve performance
  • Analyze traffic patterns and user demographics

Google Analytics collects information anonymously and reports website trends without identifying individual visitors.

Learn more: How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps

2.2 Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. This helps us:

  • Keep the educational content free for all users
  • Support ongoing development and improvements
  • Maintain and host the website

Google and its partners use cookies to:

  • Serve ads based on your visits to this site and other sites
  • Prevent the same ads from being shown too frequently
  • Detect and prevent fraud and abuse
  • Measure the effectiveness of ads

Important for Parents: We use family-friendly ad settings. Ads shown are appropriate for children and educational contexts.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us provide better service.

Types of Cookies We Use:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function (storing learning progress)
  • Analytics Cookies: Google Analytics tracking (can be opted out)
  • Advertising Cookies: Google AdSense for showing relevant ads (can be opted out)

4. Your Privacy Choices

4.1 Opt-Out of Personalized Advertising

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting:

  • Google Ads Settings
  • Digital Advertising Alliance Opt-Out
  • Network Advertising Initiative Opt-Out

4.2 Opt-Out of Google Analytics

You can prevent Google Analytics from recognizing you by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

4.3 Browser Settings

You can set your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, some features of our website may not function properly without cookies.

4.4 Clear Local Storage

To clear your learning progress and local data:

  1. Open your browser settings
  2. Navigate to Privacy or Clear Browsing Data
  3. Select "Cookies and site data" or "Local Storage"
  4. Clear data for this website

5. Children's Privacy

We are committed to protecting children's privacy.

COPPA Compliance: This website is designed for educational use by children under 13 with parental supervision. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

  • We do not require children to provide personal information to use the site
  • All learning progress is stored locally on the device
  • We do not share children's information with third parties
  • Parents can delete all locally stored data at any time
  • We use family-safe ad settings appropriate for children

Parental Control: Parents and guardians should supervise children's use of this website and can control cookie settings through their browser.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect information:

  • Data stored locally on your device is protected by your device's security
  • We use HTTPS encryption for all web traffic
  • We partner only with trusted services (Google) that maintain high security standards
  • We do not store sensitive personal information on our servers

7. Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services:

Google Analytics

  • Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy
  • Terms of Service: Google Terms

Google AdSense

  • Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy
  • Ad Settings: Manage Ad Preferences

8. International Users

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages and can be accessed globally. By using this site, you consent to the transfer of information to the United States and other countries where Google operates.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your information:

  • Access: Request information about data we collect
  • Deletion: Request deletion of locally stored data
  • Opt-Out: Opt out of analytics and personalized advertising
  • Control: Manage cookie preferences
